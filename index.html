RUNWAY MODELS BRING ENERGY TO FIRST CASTING
The energy at Work of Heart Studio was palpable as hundreds of aspiring models arrived for Fashion Week Sacramento 2026 castings. Producers praised the mix of new faces and veterans as the city welcomes its largest season ever. 

“It’s about momentum and representation,” said casting director Melissa Kay. Finalists begin rehearsals next month in preparation for the March 14 premiere at the California State Railroad Museum.
FASHION WEEK RETURNS MARCH 14 AT THE RAILROAD MUSEUM

Sacramento’s signature fashion festival returns with over 30 regional designers presenting eco‑forward collections inside the historic Railroad Museum. More than two thousand attendees are expected to take part in the weekend events. Tickets go on sale this February.
