RUNWAY MODELS TURNED OUT FOR DAY ONE CASTING

Energy and excitement filled Work of Heart Studio as DMR Productions launched the first day of Fashion Week Sacramento castings. The line of aspiring models wrapped around the block, bringing diverse talent and style to the show’s upcoming season.

Next Casting January 24 at The Atrium DMR Productions hosts a second open call for new faces at Old Sacramento’s Atrium. Organizers expect record attendance from local talent and returning applicants.
Local Designers Shine in Pre‑Show Preview Designers offered a first glimpse of their collections during a press preview event featuring sustainable materials and avant‑garde silhouettes.
FASHION WEEK RETURNS MARCH 14 AT THE RAILROAD MUSEUM The 2026 edition of Fashion Week Sacramento kicks off at the California State Railroad Museum with runway shows, exhibitions, and community panels.
