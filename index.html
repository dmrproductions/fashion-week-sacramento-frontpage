RUNWAY MODELS TURNED OUT FOR DAY ONE CASTING

Energy and excitement filled Work of Heart Studio on J Street as DMR Productions kicked off Day One of castings for Fashion Week Sacramento 2026. Stylists and judges praised the diversity and confidence of participants, setting a lively tone for the season.
