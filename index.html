RUNWAY MODELS BRING ENERGY TO FIRST CASTING
Excitement and anticipation filled the Work of Heart Studio as DMR Productions hosted its first casting for Fashion Week Sacramento 2026. Hundreds of aspiring models arrived early, eager to walk and introduce themselves to design scouts. 

"This season is about momentum," said director Melissa Kay. "We want to showcase fresh faces and bold design at a scale Sacramento has never seen before." Finalists will attend intensive rehearsal camps before the March 14 premiere at the California State Railroad Museum.
FASHION WEEK RETURNS MARCH 14 AT THE RAILROAD MUSEUM

Celebrating its 21st year, Fashion Week Sacramento will feature over 30 regional designers and sustainable collections on a runway that winds through vintage train cars. Tickets go on sale this February with proceeds supporting local art programs.
