RUNWAY MODELS IGNITE OPENING DAY CASTING
The Work of Heart Studio buzzed with anticipation as DMR Productions opened Fashion Week Sacramento 2026 castings. Aspiring models lined the block, showcasing confidence and style before a panel of local designers. Organizers say this season will highlight inclusive collections from emerging creatives throughout Northern California.
FASHION WEEK RETURNS MARCH 14 AT THE RAILROAD MUSEUM Celebrating its 21st year, Fashion Week Sacramento pairs heritage settings with modern runway shows featuring sustainable design themes. More than 2,000 guests are expected to attend events throughout Old Town and Midtown.
