FINAL CASTING FOR MODELS HELD AT THE ATRIUM

Day Two of Fashion Week Sacramento’s model castings took place at The Atrium, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit creative center for sustainability and innovation. Serving as a community incubator built on the ethos of “doing business for good,” The Atrium encourages collaboration among artists, entrepreneurs, and educators to build a kinder, more sustainable future. 

The venue houses classes, events, and art studios dedicated to social impact and sustainability. During the final casting, models of all backgrounds brought their talent and creativity to the runway call, marking the culmination of this season’s selection process. DMR Productions continues its mission of cultivating diverse voices and innovating toward an omni‑considerate future for Sacramento’s growing fashion community.
