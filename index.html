SPOTLIGHT MOMENT: Models showcase their runway presence at the model castings for Fashion Week Sacramento at Work of Heart Studios.

The energy was electric at Work of Heart Studio this morning as Fashion Week Sacramento 2026 launched its most competitive casting season yet. Before dawn broke over the capital, more than two hundred aspiring and professional models had gathered outside ready to compete for coveted spots on the March 14th runway.

This year's theme, "Fashion Reimagined - Blending Nature, Technology, and Style"" demands models who can embody both high-fashion sophistication and the authentic street culture that defines Sacramento's creative renaissance. Casting director and Producer Duane Ram noted the exceptional diversity in today's turnout, with participants ranging from seasoned professionals to first-time hopefuls discovering their runway potential.

"What we're seeing today goes beyond traditional beauty standards," Ram explained as models cycled through their walks. "We're looking for models who can not only walk but transform themselves into the designer's vision and help tell their story.""

The selection process combines technical evaluation with artistic vision. Each model poses for digital headshots, and does a basic walk and then a more dramatic walk with a llittle attidue for the designers in attendance which is also captured on camera for the out of town designers."

FASHION WEEK RETURNS MARCH 14 AT RAILROAD MUSEUM Sacramento's 21st annual Fashion Week transforms the historic California State Railroad Museum into a cutting-edge fashion showcase featuring over fifteen regional designers scheduled to take the stage and closing out the week on Saturday March 14th. The event celebrates sustainable innovation, cultural storytelling, and the dynamic intersection of technology with traditional craftsmanship.

Tickets go on sale February 1st through DMR Productions, with VIP packages offering.

Day Two Model Casing: January 24 at Atrium Gallery

Fashion Week Sacramento 2026 holds its final casting session Saturday, January 24 at Old Sacramento's Atrium Gallery. The casting runs from 11 AM to 2 PM with walk-ins welcome. Models should wear fitted black attire with minimal accessories for digital portfolio shots and runway evaluation. You don't need have an agent or have experieance. Selected participants receive spots on the March 14th runway and access to exclusive February training workshops.

Regalia in Bloom Presented as The Regalia Chamber

Regalia in Bloom, photographer Trin De's ongoing exploration of adornment as authority, presents an installation at Fashion Week Sacramento 2026 through The Regalia Chamber, a site-specific exhibition which draws from the Regalia Archives, a curated collection stewarded by Work of Heart Studio, Fashion Week Sacramento Day One model casting venue. The installation showcases De's ability to create evocative scenes while presenting a sample of the extraordinary wardrobe available for her commissioned photography work.

Emmy-Winning Production Standards

For two decades, DMR Productions has transformed Fashion Week Sacramento into a collaborative platform that unites the region's creative community. Producer Duane Ram's commitment to nurturing emerging talent extends through ongoing partnerships with academic fashion programs at Sacramento State and Delta College, providing students real-world runway experience alongside established professionals.
