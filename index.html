FINAL CASTING FOR MODELS HELD AT THE ATRIUM

Day Two of Fashion Week Sacramento’s model castings was the final round for 2026, held at The Atrium — a 501(c)(3) nonprofit creative center for sustainability and innovation. As an incubator for “business for good,” The Atrium brings together artists, entrepreneurs, and educators to build a kind and sustainable future. 

The venue houses classes, events, and art studios dedicated to collaboration and social impact. The final casting featured models from throughout California showcasing confidence and purpose as DMR Productions prepared for the upcoming runway season.
